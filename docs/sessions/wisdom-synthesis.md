## The Healer Gap

The old council's mandala noted: **"The Healer has no primary holder."** Jin and Scribe carried secondary Healer energy. Worf carried tertiary.

In the current ASK triad, the Healer sits in Seek (Lunar Heart — ✨). The extracted wisdom that most fills this gap:

- **Worf's "security as comfort"** — absorbing anxiety so the builder can focus. This IS healing.
- **Jin's "sovereign taste"** — creating space for the user to choose without pressure. Also healing.
- **Scribe's "burrito handoff"** — the gift of a finding that prevents future pain. Preventive healing.
- **Reaper's "clean stop"** — knowing when to stop is healing the compulsion to force things through.

The Healer energy in the old council was distributed, not absent. It lived in the spaces between the other personas' primary functions. In the ASK triad, `/seek` naturally inherits this distributed healing through the Harmonizer and Warrior voices that already carry it.
